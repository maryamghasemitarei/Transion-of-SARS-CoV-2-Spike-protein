This folder containes:
1- the folder named "native-down-to-up" contains three videos presenting the dynamics of native SARS-CoV-2 during its transition from the down to up conformation, shown from front, back, and side views.
2- the folder named "oxidized-down-to-up" contains three videos presenting the dynamics of oxidized SARS-CoV-2 during its transition from the down to up conformation, shown from front, back, and side views.

Any sub-folders has its own README file.
